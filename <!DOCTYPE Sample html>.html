Is Chia Seed Water Good For You?

Social media proponents claim it has digestive and weight loss benefits. Here's what experts think.

When asked if any students had examined whether chia seeds could reduce constipation, the experts said they werent aware of any. But there's good research in general showing that dietary fiber - an essential nutrient abundant in chia seeds - can help.

A two-tablespoons serving of dry chia seeds contains nearly 10 grams of fiber. That's more than twice the amount in a medium Red Delicious apple, and a good portion of the recommended 21 to 38 grams most people should eat each day.
